The restructure crate provides functionalities to match regexp patterns into struct fields. This crate only builds on rust-nightly as of now.

To use this, add the following to your Cargo.toml.

 [dependencies]
 restructure = "0.1.1"

and add this to your crate root:

 #![feature(cell_extras)]
 extern crate regex;
